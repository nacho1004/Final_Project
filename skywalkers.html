Introduction to Team

The Cheonan Hyundai Capital Skywalkers volleyball team prepared for the first time in the professional volleyball league through the new season training program "CASTLE OF SKYWALKERS," a multi-base camp in Cheonan. In addition, we have created a stronger team by conducting lectures on well-known sports leaders in Korea, and training on multimedia in order to strengthen our mental health. Hyundai Capital Volleyball Team is dedicated to volleyball culture activities in Yeonsoji, and strives to promote volleyball in the school field, and to support the players' active volleyball programs, SKYWALKERS Tour Program, etc.

Hyundai Capital Skywalkers volleyball team will do its best to create a fun volleyball culture.
